hyfe no.#

magazine

( 6 )

Hyfe is the name of the long, banching fibres of a fungus. This A6 sized magazine grows acociatively and partially uncontrolled in every direction. One editor, a word-list, and a co-author who suggest the next author makes this series grow further. Every issue is the visible outcome of this invisible network.

Hyfe is the philosophical magazine, written and edited independently by Maurice Specht. Within the riso-based publishing house Rimshot-Riso, I was partly responsible for the production and publication of the 5 first issues of this magazine.

ABOUT

Robin de haan is a graphic designer based in Rotterdam, The Netherlands, specialised in experimental book / publication design and web-development. With an interest in understanding (art) history to try to grasp the present, her book designs make use of metaphors to explore different facets of our human nature and current social landscape. Reflections, patterns and narrative devices have been recurring elements, whereas bold color contrasts, nostalgic photography and a sensibility to typography define her work.
